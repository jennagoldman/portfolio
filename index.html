About Me

I am a front-end web developer based in the Pacific Northwest. As a kid growing up in Florida, I convinced my parents not to send me to summer camp in exchange for doing something productive with my time: learning HTML. What began as a mild curiosity quickly grew to become a personal passion that I have carried with me ever sense.

I've supplemented my initial learnings over the years with front-end development courses to learn CSS, HTML5, and Bootstrap, and I have several years of professional work experience in bringing beautiful websites to life from mockups for brands such as The New York Times, Fast Company, Atlassian, Lexus, and Vogue.

I am now focusing in on JavaScript and beyond in order to bring more functionality and interactivity to my skillset.
